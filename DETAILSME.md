# 🗞️ Hyper_News – Django News Publishing Platform

Hyper_News is a role-based digital publishing platform built with Django. It simulates a real editorial workflow with article approval, journalist/editor roles, and user engagement tracking. Ideal for building a realistic newsroom app or showcasing full-stack development skills.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🧑‍💼 Role-Based Access
- **Journalists**: Create, edit, delete articles (requires approval before publishing).
- **Editors**: View and manage article submissions via a dedicated dashboard.
- **Readers**: Browse all approved articles.

### ✍️ Editorial Workflow
- Article status: Draft → Submitted → Approved/Rejected
- Editors can leave feedback or approve instantly.
- Journalists receive status updates.

### 🔍 Search & Filter
- Keyword-based search
- Filter by category, date, or author

### 📊 Article Engagement Tracking (WIP)
- Track views, clicks, and read time.
- Future: Integrate ML-powered sentiment analysis.
